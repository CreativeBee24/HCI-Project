Scenario 1: Grocery Planning

You are playing the role of a host who is expecting 6 guests and needs to prepare a lunch. You need to ensure there is enough food for 15 people in total (the 6 guests, yourself, and 3 family members). You will enter a virtual grocery store where you must plan to buy the list of ingredients needed for lunch. You must consider the cost of items such as pizza, Biryani, burger, drinks, and any desserts. You will also need to decide the quantity of each ingredient that will be needed for 15 people.
